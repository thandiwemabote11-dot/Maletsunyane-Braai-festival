Maletsunyane Braai Festival

Event Overview

The Maletsunyane Braai Festival is one of Lesotho's exciting tourism events held near the famous Maletsunyane Falls in Semonkong.The festival celebrates Basotho culture, delicious braai food, music and outdoor andventure.

Date and Location

  • Date:27-29 November 2026
  • Location:Semonkong, Maletsunyane Falls
