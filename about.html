History and Significance

The Maletsunyane Braai Festival was founded by former National University of Lesotho(NUL) students, the festive began in 2026 to showcase the rugged, breathtaking beauty of the Semonkong highlands.It is a premier three-day annual event(held in late November)that blends camping, live entertainment, and braaiing at the 192-meter Maletsunyane Falls. As Africa's highest braai(2,260m+), it boosts localtourism, creates jobs, and fosters national unity, celebrating Basotho identity and Culture.
